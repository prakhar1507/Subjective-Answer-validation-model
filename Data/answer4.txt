Water is very important in our daily life. All living things need water to survive. We drink water to stay healthy. Plants and animals also need water for their growth.

Water is found in rivers, lakes, ponds, and oceans. People use water for drinking, cooking, washing, and cleaning. It is also used in industries and agriculture.

We should save water and avoid wasting it. Clean water is necessary for good health. People in villages and cities both depend on water sources for their needs.

Therefore, water is a very essential part of life, and everyone should use it wisely. [10]
